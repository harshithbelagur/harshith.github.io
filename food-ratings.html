NYC Food Adventures

My personal rankings of the best food spots I've tried in New York City (Note: I'm vegetarian and eat eggs lol)

All Cheesecakes

★★★★★
Eileen's Special Cheesecake

The perfect balance of creamy and light, this classic New York-style cheesecake is everything you'd expect and more. The texture is incredibly smooth, and the flavor is rich without being overwhelming. The best part is the size, it's just perfect and leaves you wanting more. Everyone I've ever taken there haven't found a cheesecake better than this. You can't go wrong with any cheesecake here.
